Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

GISUGO ("we", "us", or "our") operates the GISUGO platform, which connects customers with workers for local gigs and services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you have. By using GISUGO, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile photo and personal information
  • Education level and work experience
  • Social media profile links (optional)
  • Job postings and application information
  • Chat and messaging data

2. Information From Facebook and Google Login

When you sign in with Facebook or Google, we receive basic profile information from that provider, which may include your name, email address, and profile picture, as permitted by you during sign-in. We use this information only to create and operate your GISUGO account. We do not post to your social accounts, and we do not access your contacts or friends lists.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect customers with workers
  • Process job applications and hiring
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Verify user identities

4. Information Sharing

Your profile information (such as your name, photo, and the details you choose to display) is visible to other users on the platform so that customers and workers can connect. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Firebase and Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and file storage. Your data is stored securely and is subject to Google's privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and analyze how the platform is used so we can improve it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

9. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. There are two ways to do this:

  • In the app: Open your Profile → Account settings and choose to delete your account. This removes your profile and associated personal data from our systems.
  • By email: Send a deletion request to contact@realinterfacestudios.com from the email address on your account. We will process verified requests within 30 days.

If you signed in using Facebook, you may also remove GISUGO from your Facebook account under Settings → Apps and Websites. To delete the data GISUGO holds, please use one of the methods above.

10. Children's Privacy

GISUGO is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at contact@realinterfacestudios.com.
